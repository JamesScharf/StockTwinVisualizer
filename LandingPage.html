When to use this service

So, you have a stock that you like. But, that feeling is based on more than the stock price. You like what newspapers say about it online. Or, maybe you like the gist of its Wikipedia page. Either way, this service helps you retrieve stocks based on internet content similarity. However, be aware that some companies lack strong online presences, so these comparisons might be incomplete or uninformed.

How to use this service

In the search box in the top right, input the stock symbol (e.g. "GOOG"), followed by a number for the sample size from which we initially select stocks. Your results will improve with larger samples, but be advised that for additional stocks your computation time increases by about 30 seconds. Here are some examples:

    GOOG 4
    AAPL 8
    MSFT 11

Start off with a low sample size and increase only if you need to.
